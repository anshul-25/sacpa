SACPA

Student Analysis and Co-curricular Prediction Analysis

About

This product is concentrated towards the academicians and coordinators of educational institutions to map and predict the progress of students and peers, thus providing data to develop extra progressive programs and curriculum in order to meet globalised standards and to create a healthy learning environment




Student Information

Students are classified into first class, second class or fail on the basis of academic performance considering their previous records and involvement in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. It also shows the profile of each student.




Reports and Graphs

Consolidated reports based on academic and attendance status of each student. Shows consolidated reports on demographic data based on attendance and academics. It also shows if there is any relation between a student's academic marks and attendance percentage. Co-curricular and Extra-curricular activty reports are also present.




Placement Report

Reports and graphs related to placement records. It also shows the percentage qualification rate per company as well as per student. It aims at recognizing student's weak spots and helps management to train the students accordingly.




Academic Information

It contains graphs to compare a student's academic marks and attendance percentage for each semester for each subject as well as for all consolidated semesters.
